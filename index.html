    Technical Writing

    Due to the nature of technical writing, much of my work is with internal documentation and material covered by NDAs and cannot be made public. The following works are related to my graduate degree work at University of Strausborg TCLoc program.

    A Meta-Analysis of Structured Documentation in Virtual Spaces
    Theoretical Ethics Thesis Developed for University of Strausborg TCLoc Master's

    This exploratory research thesis challenges the conventional notion of audience in technical writing within virtual spaces. Focusing on the evolving landscape of authorship in digital environments, I put forward the question of the centrality of human audiences as primary criteria for authorship in technical communications.
    The article advocates a reevaluation of ethical responsibilities, controls, and publication processes in response to the transformation brought about by tools like XML and semantic segmentation.
